\noindent\textbf{Quality score composition.} The MetaExpert evaluates four dimensions, each scored 0--1:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \textbf{Correctness} (weight 0.4): Factual accuracy against the source chunk.
    \item \textbf{Completeness} (weight 0.3): Coverage of all relevant aspects in the chunk.
    \item \textbf{Relevance} (weight 0.2): Alignment between instruction and response.
    \item \textbf{Structure} (weight 0.1): Clarity of formatting and logical flow.
\end{itemize}
The composite score is the weighted sum. Records with score $<0.6$ trigger a feedback round; records that still fail after $R=2$ rounds are discarded.
